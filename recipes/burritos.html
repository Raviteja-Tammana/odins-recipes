Burrito



Description

Burritos are tortillas wrapped around a mixed filling of such ingredients as meat, cheese, beans, and vegetables. They differ from the taco in that the circular tortilla in the latter case is simply folded, not wrapped.



Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Refried Beans
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Rice
  • Pico de Gallo

Steps

  1. Lay Out Tortilla
  2. Include Ingredients One-By-One
  3. Fold and Wrap Burrito
