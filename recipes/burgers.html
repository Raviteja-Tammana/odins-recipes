Burger



Description

Burgers are any cut buns with a hot filling, including vegetables, meat, and cheese.



Ingredients

  • Sesame Buns
  • Slice of Cheese
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Vegetable Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Toast the Buns
  2. Include Ingredients One-By-One
  3. Press the Buns Together
